Notes on “all possible characters”

Bytes 0–31 and 127 are control codes in ASCII; they render as names here (NUL, LF, ESC…). Bytes 128–255 are shown using Latin-1 mapping for a stable 1:1 byte→glyph display. This is for recoverability training.
