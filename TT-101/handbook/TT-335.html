TT-335 — Morse Game 3: Encode + Transmit (Operator Challenge)

> “The most dangerous phrase is ‘we’ve always done it this way.’”

> — Grace Hopper

---

**Goal:** learn to *encode* into Morse and transmit consistently (light, sound, tap).

Equipment (choose one)

  • flashlight / phone flashlight
  • pen tapping on a table
  • a simple beep generator

Timing Setup

Pick a unit time **T** you can hold reliably:

  • Beginner: ~250–350 ms per dit
  • Intermediate: ~120–180 ms per dit

Encode These Messages (write the Morse)

1) `TT-101`

2) `TIME BEACON`

3) `CONTINUITY`

Write your Morse using `.` and `-`, separating letters by spaces and words by `/`.

Transmit One Message

Transmit message #2 (“TIME BEACON”) using your chosen medium.

Self-Verification

Record your transmission and attempt to decode it back.

If you cannot record, have another person decode it live.

Pass Condition

  • You can encode all 3 correctly.
  • Your transmitted message decodes back with **≥ 90% accuracy**.
