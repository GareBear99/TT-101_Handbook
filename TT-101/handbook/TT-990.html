TT-990 — Binary Decoder Dictionary (0–255)

A compact reference mapping every 8‑bit value (0–255) to decimal, hex, binary, and a safe character label. Use this when decoding QR slate blocks or training with the DIY games.

DecHexBinASCIILatin-1Note

Tip: Control bytes (0–31, 127) are shown by name. Extended bytes (128–255) follow ISO‑8859‑1 labels where printable.
