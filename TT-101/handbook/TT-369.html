TT-369 — QR Block Practice (Decode → Verify)

This is a training + verification surface for TT-315 slates. You load a slate.json, then paste a single QR block payload (as HEX or base64). The tool computes SHA-256 of the bytes and checks against the expected per-block hash.

1) Load slate.json

Must contain blocks.sha256 (packer v1.1+).

2) Paste a block

Notes

  • QR scanners often output bytes already; if your scanner exports as base64, use base64 mode.
  • HEX accepts whitespace and newlines; they are ignored.
  • This does not reconstruct the whole payload — it verifies a single block matches the expected bytes.
