## Quick Decode Rules
1) A letter is a sequence of `.` and `-`.
2) Letters are separated by a longer pause (≈ 3× a dit).
3) Words are separated by an even longer pause (≈ 7× a dit).
4) When in doubt, write the pattern down first, then map to the table.

## Space / Word Break
Represent word breaks as:
- `/` in written form, or
- a wide gap (≈ 7T) in signaling form

Example: `TIME / BEACON`
