# TT-334 — Morse Game 2: Word Decoder (Phrases + Spacing)

> “Knowledge grows through criticism.”
> — Karl Popper

---

**Goal:** decode words and phrases using spacing rules. This is the Morse equivalent of the Binary Crossword.

## How to Play
- Letters are separated by spaces.
- Words are separated by `/`.

Decode the following:

### Puzzle 1
`- .. -- .`

### Puzzle 2
`- --- -.- . -.`

### Puzzle 3
`- .. -- . / -... . .- -.-. --- -.`

### Puzzle 4
`- - / .---- ----- .----`

### Puzzle 5
`-- --- .-. ... . / -.-. --- -.. .`

## Difficulty Upgrade
Decode this without writing every letter first:

### Puzzle 6
`.- .-. -.-. .... .. ...- . / .--. .-. --- - --- -.-. --- .-..`
