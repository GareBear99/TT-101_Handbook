# TT-366 Binary Crossword Puzzle (DIY Handbook Game)

## Concept Summary
A crossword-style mini-game that trains **binary decoding** relevant to the QR-slate workflow.
Clues are provided as **8-bit ASCII byte strings**, and the player fills a crossword grid offline in the handbook.

## Operational Implications
- Reinforces byte/ASCII familiarity used when recovering QR slate blocks.
- Provides a low-cost training activity that survives printing/export.
