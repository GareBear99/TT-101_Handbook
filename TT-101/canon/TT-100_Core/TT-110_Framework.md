# TT-110 — Epistemic Constraint Discipline

## Thesis
No continuity model may exceed its epistemic limits without becoming speculative.

## Constraints
- No cosmological assumptions
- No multiverse requirement
- No determinism requirement
- No retrocausality assertion

Constraint-first design preserves falsifiability.
